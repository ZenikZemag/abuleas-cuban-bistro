Our Story

Abuela's Cuban Bistro was born from a love for family, food, and the rich culture of Cuba. Our founder, inspired by his grandmother's cooking, wanted to create a space that felt like home—a place where every meal is a celebration and every guest is family.

We use only the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes to bring you the authentic taste of Cuba. From our family to yours, ¡Buen Provecho!
